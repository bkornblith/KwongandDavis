Our Story

Built on a belief
that everyone deserves
a fair chance

Kwong & Davis LLP was founded by two immigration attorneys who met while representing clients at the New York Legal Assistance Group — one of the nation's largest legal services providers. United by a shared belief that immigration law should be a force for equity, Julia and Brendan launched the firm to bring the same depth of public-interest experience to a wider community of clients.

The firm's practice spans the full breadth of US immigration law, with particular expertise in humanitarian cases, asylum proceedings, and the special needs of Australian nationals navigating the US visa system. Every client is treated as a whole person, not a case number.

Practice Areas

How we can
help you

Our practice spans the full spectrum of US immigration law, with deep expertise in humanitarian protection and cross-border matters for those with ties to Australia.

⚖️
Asylum & Humanitarian Protection

Representing individuals who have fled persecution based on race, religion, nationality, political opinion, or membership in a particular social group. We handle affirmative and defensive asylum cases with sensitivity and rigor.

🌏
E-3 Visas & Australian Nationals

The E-3 visa is available exclusively to Australian citizens in specialty occupations. We help Australians navigate the US visa process — from initial applications to renewals, transfers, and status changes.

👨‍👩‍👧
Family-Based Immigration

Petitions to reunite immediate relatives and qualifying family members with their US citizen or lawful permanent resident sponsors. We guide you through every step of the petition and adjustment process.

🛡️
Removal Defense & Appeals

Representation before immigration courts and the Board of Immigration Appeals. We fight to protect our clients from deportation and exhaust every available legal avenue for relief.

🌿
Green Cards & Adjustment

Guiding clients through employment-based, family-based, and diversity-based pathways to lawful permanent residence — including adjustment of status and consular processing.

📋
DACA, TPS & Special Visas

Renewals and applications for Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals, Temporary Protected Status, special immigrant juvenile status, U visas for crime victims, and T visas for trafficking survivors.

Our Attorneys

Meet the partners

JK
Julia Kwong
Partner & Co-Founder

Julia is a seasoned immigration attorney with deep roots in public interest law. Before co-founding Kwong & Davis LLP, she served as a Senior Staff Attorney at the New York Legal Assistance Group (NYLAG), where she represented low-income clients in complex immigration matters including asylum, removal defense, and family petitions.

Known for her meticulous preparation and genuine care for clients, Julia has successfully litigated before immigration courts, the Board of Immigration Appeals, and federal circuit courts. She is fluent in Mandarin and has a particular focus on protecting clients from East and Southeast Asia.
